Punishments

Three Strike System

First Strike

  • Issued for first-time violations
  • Includes breaking server rules as staff
  • Results in formal warning
  • Lasts for 14 days

Second Strike

  • Repeating same violations
  • May result in temporary restrictions
  • Lasts for 14 days
  • Requires review with leadership

Third Strike

  • Continued violations
  • Results in removal from staff team
  • May be appealed after 30 days
  • Severe violations may skip strikes
