Staff Policies

Privacy Protection

In order to preserve our user's privacy and uphold strict ethical standards, all staff must avoid collecting any personally identifiable information unless absolutely necessary and explicitly consented to.

Protected Information Includes:

  • Full name or any identifying aliases
  • Physical address or location details
  • Phone numbers
  • Email addresses
  • Identification numbers (SSN, driver's license, etc.)
  • Financial information
  • Any other information that could be used to identify or contact the user

Activity Requirements

To maintain an active and responsive support team, we require all staff members to remain engaged with our community.

Key Requirements:

  • 14-day inactivity without warning may result in strike, demotion, or removal
  • Participation in community discussions is encouraged
  • Extended absences should be communicated to leadership

Example Scenario:

A staff member hasn't responded to any tickets or participated in server discussions for 3 weeks without notice. After attempts to contact them go unanswered, they would be subject to removal from the staff team.

We understand that real life takes priority, so communication about availability is always appreciated.

Community Engagement

In order to maintain activity, as well as good public reputation:

  • Greet new members in #general
  • Run the !support command to direct new members to ticket creation
  • Active moderators help deter bad activity
  • Participation in technical discussions builds community trust

Specialist Relations

Do NOT disrespect the specialists. They are the backbone of the server.

  • While you may have more permissions than them, you are NOT better than/more important than specialists
  • The moderator and specialist systems are independent but follow similar rules
  • Do not expect different treatment based on your position
  • Collaboration between staff and specialists is essential for quality support
