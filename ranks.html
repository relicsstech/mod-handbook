Staff Ranks

Junior Moderator

Requirements:

Entry-level position obtained after successfully applying and being accepted into our staff team.

Purpose:

This probationary period allows you to demonstrate your moderational skills, server activity, ability to follow protocols, and commitment to the community.

Permissions:

  • Manage Nicknames
  • Timeout members
  • Delete messages
  • Mute members in VC

Moderator

Requirements:

  • Minimum 2 weeks as Junior Moderator
  • Consistent rule enforcement
  • Positive community feedback
  • Approval from senior staff

Purpose:

Full moderation capabilities to maintain server order, handle common disciplinary actions, and assist junior staff members.

Permissions:

All Junior Moderator permissions plus:

  • Kick members
  • Deafen and Move members in VC
  • Access to moderation tools

Junior Administrator

Requirements:

  • Minimum 2 months as Moderator
  • Demonstrated leadership skills
  • Excellent decision-making record
  • Unanimous senior staff approval

Purpose:

Transitional leadership role to prepare for full administrative duties, including handling complex situations and mentoring junior staff.

Permissions:

All Moderator permissions plus:

  • View audit log
  • Mention @here and @everyone
  • Pin messages
  • Access to admin channels

Administrator

Requirements:

  • Minimum 2 months as Junior Administrator
  • Exceptional leadership demonstrated
  • Perfect disciplinary record
  • Founder approval

Purpose:

Full administrative authority to oversee server operations, handle sensitive matters, and implement server-wide changes.

Permissions:

All Junior Administrator permissions plus:

  • Ban members
  • Create and Manage events
  • Server configuration access
  • Staff management privileges

Head of Staff

Description:

The Head of Staff has all permissions and oversees all staff promotions and demotions. Only one Head of Staff serves at a time.

Current Head of Staff:

Ray

Responsibilities:

  • Final say in staff decisions
  • Direct communication with founders
  • Server policy development
  • Staff performance evaluations
  • Conflict resolution
